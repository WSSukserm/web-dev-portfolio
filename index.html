Who, me?

Something enticed me into the tech field. Unfortunately, it wasn’t the same kind of cookies. But, I did make my venture inside this magically infinite world after earning my degree in Journalism.

It began as a knack for writing. Then, one WordPress blog, 99 Instagram posts, and a Tumblr full of aesthetically beautiful images later, I realized that much of my spare time is spent creating and composing.

The next step was only a natural inclination to create a fully functioning, responsive website where I could share this collective hobby. In that, madness ensued (not really - sane madness of sorts).

Front-end web development opened up a once limited world of click-to-post to a surprising interest in coding where I can apply both my creativity and meticulousness in a field I couldn’t be more curious about.

Hand-Coded HTML

My focus is writing clean, well-formatted, semantic HTML5 by hand to make sure that the content is easy to read, easy to collaborate, trouble-shoot, and accessible.

Well-Organized CSS

I pride myself on writing CSS that is easy to read and build on. I focus on keeping my CSS lean and fast to load, and I make it a habit to stay up to date on current best practices.

Converting Designs into Code

You can trust me to take a designer's PSD and quickly & accurately convert it into a webpage that is pixel-perfect match.

That's it?

Nope! There's always more learning to do and more skills to build. Currently, I'm learning about responsive web development. After that, I'll be on to JavaScript!
